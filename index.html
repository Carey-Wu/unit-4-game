Maester of the Citadel Game


You are the newest Maester at the Citadel. As such, it is your responsiblity to treat the sick of the 7 kingdoms, and in particular, those infected with Grayscale.

Each patient's infection level has a number rating assigned to it. There are a number of treatment options below that each have a number randomly assigned to them. The patients react differently to different treatments so the values will change per patient.

Select the treatment options to match your score to that of the patient's illness. You save the patient by matching your score to the exact score of the patient's illness. If you go over, the patient dies.

After each patient, the treatment options will reset to a new number.
